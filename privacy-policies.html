1. Our Approach to Privacy

We build privacy-first apps. We collect only what is needed to make our Apps work, we keep it to a minimum, and we never sell your personal information to anyone.

2. How Our Apps Work (Mostly Offline)

Most ODN & Sons Apps are designed to work mostly offline. The data you create is stored locally on your own device, so it stays with you and is not sent to our servers for everyday use.

Our Apps are "semi-online" — they only connect to the internet for a small number of features:

  • Purchases — to process payments or unlock premium features.
  • Feedback — when you choose to send us feedback through our website.

We also generally do not rely on third-party services inside our Apps. Where any external service is used, it is limited to the online features above (for example, an app store or payment provider to complete a purchase).

3. Information We Collect

Data stored on your device

For most Apps, the content and information you create is stored locally on your device. It stays under your control and is not transmitted to us.

Information you provide online

When you use one of our limited online features, we may process the details you choose to give us — for example, the information needed to complete a purchase, or the message and email address you include when you send feedback through our website.

Information collected automatically

We keep automatic collection to a minimum. At most, this may include basic device and app information (such as device type, operating system version and app version) needed to deliver an online feature or keep it secure.

4. How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate and maintain our Apps.
  • To process purchases and unlock premium features.
  • To respond to feedback and support requests you send us.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Because our Apps run mostly on your device and rarely use external services, sharing is minimal. Where it is necessary to deliver an online feature — for example, an app store or payment provider completing a purchase — only the limited information required is shared. We may also disclose information where required by law.

6. Third-Party Services

We generally do not use third-party services within our Apps. The few cases where an external service is involved are limited to our online features (such as a payment provider for purchases). Those providers handle data under their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information against unauthorised access, loss or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device remains for as long as you keep it, and you can delete it at any time from within the App. Any information we receive through an online feature is kept only for as long as needed for that purpose, or as required by applicable law.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

10. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete or restrict the use of your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of our Apps after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please reach us at support@allcreatormind.com or through our contact page.
