Privacy Policy

Applies to all ODN & Sons apps · Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ODN & Sons ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the mobile applications and services we provide (each, an "App"). ODN & Sons was established in 2022 and is registered in Hisar, India. This single policy applies to every App we publish, now and in the future.

1. Our Approach to Privacy

We build privacy-first apps. We collect only what is needed to make our Apps work, we keep it to a minimum, and we never sell your personal information to anyone.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

Some Apps let you create content or an account. In those cases we may process details you choose to give us, such as an email address or the data you enter into the App.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic device and app information (such as device type, operating system version and app version).
  • Anonymous usage and diagnostic data that helps us fix issues and improve performance.

Data stored on your device

Many of our Apps store your data locally on your device. Where that is the case, that data remains under your control and is not transmitted to us.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate and maintain our Apps.
  • To improve, personalise and develop new features.
  • To diagnose technical problems and ensure security and stability.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers (for example, hosting, analytics or crash-reporting providers) only to the extent necessary to operate our Apps, and only under appropriate confidentiality obligations. We may also disclose information where required by law.

5. Third-Party Services

Some Apps may use third-party services (such as analytics, cloud storage or payment processors). These services handle data according to their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information against unauthorised access, loss or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as it is needed for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete or restrict the use of your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of our Apps after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please reach us at support@odnandsons.com or through our contact page.
